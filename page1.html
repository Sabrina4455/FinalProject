Something I learned this year was how to work with Python Turtle Graphics.

Here is the code:

import turtle
turtle = turtle.Turtle()

turtle.pencolor("pink")

NUM_CIRCLES = 50  
RADIUS = 125        
ANGLE = 80           
ANIMATION_SPEED = 10  

turtle.speed(ANIMATION_SPEED)

for x in range(NUM_CIRCLES):
    turtle.circle(RADIUS)
    turtle.right(ANGLE)
